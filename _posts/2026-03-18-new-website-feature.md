I've just finished adding a new feature to the website. All gallery pieces now have a link icon in the bottom left corner. 
Clicking on it will being you to the artwork's page where you can read the descirption, give the image a like, maybe even comment on it if I figure out how to install comments on a static site without selling our personal data to Disqus. It also makes the artwork bookmarkable, so you can save it in your browser or send it to a friend. /
It took me a lot of work to restructure all these artworks and pages, but I think it's much nicer now. /
Enjoy!
