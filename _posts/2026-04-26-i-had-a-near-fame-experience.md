Last week someone found my Overwatch comic and reposted it on Reddit. For two days it was in the spotlight, and I got quite a few visits on my website from there. It felt good, especially considering that I have always been too shy to post my art on Reddit. It was cool to see that people liked it in there.

Currently I am working on some things, both on my website and on things related to The Snake Queen. I am collecting some ideas for the final chapter. If I get too many, I may split it in two parts. I don't want to go above 5k words, and I probably will, judging by the sheer number of notes I am taking.

I am also preparing some materials for the spin-off mini comic, Feng: Before Dusk. I will put the cover preview on my Ko-fi page if you want to see it ahead of time.

Trying to distract myself from the overwhelming feeling of sadness that I experience from almost finishing the novel, I am making a lot of extra content with my OCs to keep them a little longer. I already picked the next novel to write, but I can't get over my adventure ending with Raajimera, Feng, and Zephyr. I just love them so much. I stalled the novel for long enough due to this reason.
This will be the first story project I ever finished. My brain is not wired for this kind of departure :(
