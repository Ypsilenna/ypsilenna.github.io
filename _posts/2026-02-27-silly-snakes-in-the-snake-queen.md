I imagined something totally silly. Picture that: an alternative version of The Snake Queen where the all-powerful snake spirit animal is actually a ball python. Imagine Raa, the terrifying deity whose face looks like the :3 emoji. <br>

That would be hilarious. I'm not gonna do it, but it would be funny.<br>

One person asked me why I don't want to change Raa to a ball python for comedic value. I think I don't want this story to be silly, and ball pythons don't seem like the kind of snakes you would consider fearsome.<br>

Raa is actually a blotched royal/diadem snake (Spalerosophis diadema). I made her purple for magic reasons, but she's supposed to look like one of these.<br>

The character in this story that is actually supposed to be silly is Feng, who is a glossy-bellied-racer (platyceps ventromaculatus). Unfortunately that character also couldn't be a ball python due to the size differences. Feng is supposed to fit into Raajimera's pocket. * sigh * , no ball pythons in this story for me, it seems.
