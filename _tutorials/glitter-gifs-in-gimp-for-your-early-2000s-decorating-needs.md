Putting glitter on my doodles is one of my favorite things, and I remember being confused about it when I was a teen, so I decided to make a small guide for making them. 

Before I start, however, I would like to point out that this is not a tutorial that requires you to follow it 100%. These are just guidelines to help you start making glitter GIFs. 
I usually texture them in Krita or other software I'm comfortable using and just compile them in GIMP. For the sake of simplicity, I will make the whole tutorial for GIMP.

<b>Why in GIMP?</b>

Because GIMP by far has the easiest and the most straight-forward way of saving GIFs. You don't actually have to animate anything, just put every frame as a separate layer, save it a .gif and select "as animation". That's all, easy!<br><br>
