Ever since I decided to take a break from posting art online, I started buying traditional art supplies, and now I am obsessed. I got myself a box of cool alcohol markers! Traditional art is so much fun.<br>

<img src="/static/image/blog/markers.jpg"><br>

I feel that traditional art feels more personal, I don't know if you guys can relate.<br>
I simply love drawing my original characters on paper. <br>

I think that my digital art still looks slightly better, but it also doesn't have all the things that annoy me in digital. For example, it's much easier to draw the line art and much harder to accidentally draw on the wrong layer (you can't confuse layers if you only have one. See? Genius 😂)<br>

We shall see how long it will be for me to start missing digital, though. I already accidentally ended up closing traditional lines thinking "I got to close this line for the bucket tool."<br>

I guess you can bring a digital artist out of digital art, but you can't bring digital art out of the digital artist.
