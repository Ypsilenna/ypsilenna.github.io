Today I'm going to talk about several topics. One of which is me helping my partner make a game for a game jam. All I could do was add some doodles and help him with writing, but the game is already submitted and playable <a href="https://juanjosalvador.itch.io/do-not-make-any-noise">here</a>.<br>
It's not a lot, but it was just a quick project for the game jam. He wants us to enter another one soon, so you will see more mini-games illustrated by me!

The second thing is that I am currently in the process of writing chapter 13 (and the epilogue) of The Snake Queen, and it should be shipped between one and two weeks. This chunk will close the novel. I am both happy and sad about it. Happy because this is my first finished story, and I will finally wash away the label of a comic artist who started a lot and finished nothing. Sad because I picked this story because it was supposed to be short and easy. I didn't even like Raajimera when I started, and now the vision of letting them go makes me upset. I will still create some small side content with the characters. There will be the mini comic about Feng that I already announced, one-shot mini comics based on the events in the novel, possibly some self-contained extra chapters, illustrations... It won't be the same, though.  
I hope you enjoy it nonetheless.

The third thing is that my partner and I adopted two adorable kittens about three weeks ago, and I absolutely love them. Visit my Mastodon/Bluesky profile for some pics; they are so cute! Their names are Rosalina and Peach after the Mario princesses. They recently started sleeping in the same room with us, and they love it. They are much calmer now.

The fourth thing is that I have already mentioned that I am thinking of discontinuing the Side7 mirror. I may actually permanently leave that place. I am considering taking the novel down from there. It's for reasons I would rather not disclose here, but I'm feeling quite bad in there lately.

The fifth thing is, I already mentioned this one on my Ko-fi page, but the next novel in the queue is called Little Star and the City of Wonders. It's about a child-hating mechanic who made some rather questionable life choices. After many years, he tries to start a new adventure to run away from the flashbacks only to accidentally get stuck having to take care of a kid who claims she's a princess of a magical island somewhere in the middle of the sea.  
The story will be a dramedy just like The Snake Queen is, and the characters will have to face their traumas and attempt to heal in suboptimal conditions. The mechanic finds out that the Crescent Moon Isle is real but also actively terrorised by the father of the princess, who turned into a literal monster.  
I will write the first draft before publishing and wait until I have at least several chapters to show you, so it will take me a while to introduce the next novel.

There are already two more novels lined up after this one, but I will wait until Little Star is started to announce them. I think all of them will be dramedies, though. I think I would explode if I had to be bound either to pure comedy or to pure drama. If you enjoy my Polish deadpan and funky writing "accent", you will probably enjoy the other stories too. There should be about 12 stories in total if I write them before departing from this world, so there will always be content to return to on this website.

Buuut… This blog is already longer than it was supposed to be. Until the next one!
