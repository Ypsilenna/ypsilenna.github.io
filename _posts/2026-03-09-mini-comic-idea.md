Lately, I've been thinking of making a small spin-off comic about the snake companion from *The Snake Queen*. The title would probably be something like *Feng: Before Dusk*, and it would be about the life of that character before they answered the call of the snake goddess.

People who read some of the chapters in early access loved Feng, and I think the mini-comic could be super funny!

I can already imagine some panel, such as the day Feng hatched or the first hunt of Feng. So many possibilities!

I hope I won't forget about it because the novel takes more than it was supposed to due to mental health reasons... 🙄
