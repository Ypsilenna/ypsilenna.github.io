This is Riamidea before she was assassinated by the Order of the White Crow. She used to be one of the most powerful sorcerers. After her sister got her resurrected with the magic of the night dragons, she could only wield unholy necromantic magic. She never lost her sense of style, though.

Watercolors, acrylics, and colored pencils.
