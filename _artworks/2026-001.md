The first full illustration I made traditionally. The background is made with watercolors and glitter pens. The rest is done with colored pencils.<br>
The colors are a bit off because I was still trying to figure out how to make colored illustrations traditionally. <br>
As you can see, the edges are a bit jaggy. That's because I didn't buy a proper masking tape, and instead used some old one that I found in my partner's belongings. Luckily now I got a proper equipment for doing this kind of job, so... hooray!<br>
As for the artwork itself, Rallosameer wears a back piece called Cape of Nightmares. It connects him to the dimension of dreams. I had this idea that he could grab escaping nightmares before they are fully on the loose. The nightmare eyes are drawn with glitter pens, and they are based on the nightmares I had as a child. I was terrified of eyeballs. Sometimes they still creep me out.<br>
