Marcy is a wannabe illusionist. Together with her best friend, Mistletoe, they are motivated to create the greatest show this world had ever seen! Life wasn’t always so easy for her though. Ever since she was a little guinea pig, she dreamed of being a magician. Almost her entire family mocked her for it… almost. Her beloved wealthy auntie Kalina always believed in lil’ Marcy. It’s time to make her proud!
