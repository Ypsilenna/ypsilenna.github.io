I managed to make it match the color scheme of the site, so they display properly both in the default light mode and in the dark mode that I put there to make reading novels easier! I also made some other minor changes around the site; you can see them in the changelog on the bottom of the page.

I swear, I grew my website so much that my partner is getting a headache when he sees the structure of it because I just decided to make it for everything, haha.
